Goals of the association
  • Facilitate the integration of individuals into society by promoting their rights and obligations, promoting social and cultural activities, and improving language and professional skills.
  • Support and encourage activities to improve the situation of its members.
  • Cooperate with institutes, organizations and individuals in Switzerland that can help it achieve its goals.
