Under the name "Ensemble" (Together), in accordance with Article 60 et seq. Of the Swiss Civil Law, a non-profit cultural and social association with headquarters in Neuchâtel is established.
The actions of the association will be conducted in a social and cultural context.
The association does not tolerate any discrimination for geographical, racial or religious affiliation.
